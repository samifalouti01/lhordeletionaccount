How to Delete Your Account in l-Hor

Open the app

Launch the l-Hor application on your device.

Go to Profile

Navigate to your profile section by tapping on the menu icon usually located at top left of the screen, and click on Profile icon.

Click on Settings icon

Find and tap on the settings icon typically represented by a gear or cog symbol.

Tap on Delete Account

Scroll down in the settings menu to find the "Delete Account" option and tap on it.

Confirm deletion

Follow any additional prompts to confirm that you want to delete your account. You may need to enter your password or provide other verification.

Important: This will permanently delete your account and associated data from our servers. This action cannot be undone, so please make sure you want to proceed with account deletion.
